Our Charity Programs

We run various programs designed to address different needs in our communities, all based on Islamic principles of charity and social welfare.

Food for Families

Providing nutritious meals to underprivileged families during Ramadan and throughout the year, following the Sunnah of feeding the hungry.

Learn More

Islamic Education

Sponsoring Islamic education for children in need, ensuring they learn the Quran and Sunnah while receiving quality secular education.

Learn More

Water for Life

Building wells and water systems in drought-affected areas, fulfilling the noble act of providing clean water as ongoing charity (Sadaqah Jariyah).

Learn More
