Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our foundation and operations

How can I donate to GENEROUS GIVER FOUNDATION?

You can donate through our website using our secure donation form, via Mobile Money (MoMo) by dialing *170# and using our merchant code 030059, or through bank transfer to our official accounts. We also accept in-person donations at any of our offices.

How can I volunteer with your organization?

We welcome volunteers in various capacities. You can fill out our volunteer application form on our website or visit any of our offices to register. We have opportunities in program implementation, administration, fundraising, and community outreach.

How is my donation used?

We are committed to transparency. Approximately 85% of all donations go directly to our programs (food distribution, education, water projects, etc.), while 15% covers essential administrative and fundraising costs. You can specify which program you'd like your donation to support.

Can I visit your projects in person?

Yes, we welcome visitors to our project sites. For security and logistical reasons, we request that you contact us in advance to arrange a visit. Our team will be happy to show you our work and introduce you to beneficiaries when appropriate.

How can my organization partner with yours?

We welcome partnerships with organizations that share our values and mission. Please contact our partnership team through the contact form or email partnerships@generousgiverfoundation.org with details about your organization and proposed collaboration.
